Max Tempurature

As suspected, the temperature recorded in cities increase as you approach the Equator (i.e. 0 Latitude).
Additionally, you can see that cities in the souther hemisphere (neg (-) latitude) have higher temperatures, which would indicate that the norther hemisphere is going through their winter season while the southern is going through their summer.
